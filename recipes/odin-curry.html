Presenting: The Allfather's Curry

Vegan Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

This dish is sure to spice you right up with its unique seasoning profile, not to mention the meld of textures genereated from the hearty tuber (sweet potato) and chickpeas.

A cuisine like this is popular in a world over, which makes this an advneture's classic find. Odin couldn't keep this one to himself, so he's sharing it with you here.


Ingredients


  • 3 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 (15-oz) can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 (14.5-oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (14-oz) can coconut milk
  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chile flakes
  • 1 cup baby spinach

How To Put It All Together:


  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and sweet potato. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.

  2. Season with garam masala, cumin, turmeric, salt, and chile flakes. Add spinach right before serving.
